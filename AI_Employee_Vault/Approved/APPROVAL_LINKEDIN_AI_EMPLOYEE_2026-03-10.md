## LinkedIn Post Draft

I just built something that's been on my mind for a while: a Personal AI Employee that actually works 24/7.

Here's what it does:
- Monitors my Gmail and WhatsApp for urgent messages
- Drafts responses based on my communication style
- Flags payments and important decisions for my approval
- Updates my dashboard with real-time status
- Never sleeps, never takes a break

The tech stack: Claude Code as the reasoning engine, Obsidian for memory/state management, Python watchers for monitoring, and Playwright for browser automation.

The key insight? AI agents need three things: perception (watchers), reasoning (Claude), and action (MCP servers). Plus a human-in-the-loop for anything sensitive.

This is what "AI Employee" really means in 2026 - not replacing humans, but handling the repetitive stuff so we can focus on what matters.

What would you automate first if you had a personal AI assistant?
